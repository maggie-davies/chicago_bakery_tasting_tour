Best Bakers Tasting Trail: Chicago 2026

In July, my brother is competing in Chicago's Best Baker Contest, the city's only amateur baking competition. Inspired by the show The Bear, I decided to develop a list of places my brother can go to cultivate his palette, gain exposure to different flavors, and spark ideas for his own entries. In order to be eligible to win Best Baker, bakers have to register for at least five categories of baked goods. My brother is therefore planning to submit a Bread, a Cake, Cookies, a Pastry, and a Pie.

Click the markers on the map below to explore places I recommend my brother visit on his tasting tour, and enter the world of Chicago baking with us!
