Bakery Tasting Trail: Chicago 2026

This map was made for the author's brother, who will be participating in Chicago's amateur city-wide baking contest in July 2026. Inspired by the show The Bear, I researched places my brother can go to cultivate his palette and spark ideas for his own creations. Each location offers something unique.
